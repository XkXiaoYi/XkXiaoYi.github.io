The following information are provided for each isotope in the database.

  • Abundance - Per cent abundance of isotope
  • Sigma(Old) - Total thermal (n,g) cross section from 1981MuZQ, 1984MuZY, and 2003MuZZ.
  • Sigma(New) - Total observed thermal (n,g) cross section from final Adopted data.
  • Data - Revised ENSDF, Budapest, and final Adopted data in ENSDF format. Read with Isotope Explorer software.
  • Intensity Balances - Gamma-ray intensity balance through the level scheme.
  • Energy fits - Least-squares fit of gamma-ray energy to the level scheme.
  • ENSDF/Budapest Comparison - Matching of Budapest and ENSDF data.
  • Comparison of Adopted Data with Budapest, ENSDF, LANL, and Lone databases - including a statistical comparison of each dataset renormalized to the Adopted data intensity scale.
References:
  • 1981MuZQ: S.F.Mughabghab, M.Divadeenam, N.E.Holden, Neutron Cross Sections, Vol.1, Neutron Resonance Parameters and Thermal Cross Sections, Part A, Z = 1-60, Academic Press, New York (1981).
  • 1984MuZY: S.F.Mughabghab, Neutron Cross Sections, Vol.1, Neutron Resonance Parameters and Thermal Cross Sections, Part B, Z = 61-100, Academic Press, New York (1984).
  • 2003MuZZ: S.F.Mughabghab, Thermal Neutron Capture Cross Sections, Resonance Integrals, and g-factors, INDC(NDS)-440 (2003).

The following databases are provided.

  • Lone et al Database (Text)
  • Reedy and Frankle (LANL) database
  • PGAA Prompt Gammas from ENSDF (Normalized to Mughabghab Cross Sections)
  • PGAA Prompt Gammas from Budapest
  • PGAA Prompt Gammas from final Adopted data
  • PGAA Decay Gammas from final Adopted data
